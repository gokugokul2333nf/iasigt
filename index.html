Welcome to IASIGT 2025

The First International Conference on “Implication of AI for Sustainable Innovations in Global Transition (IASIGT – 2025)” will highlight the latest breakthroughs and innovations in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, sustainable practices, Electrical, Electronics, and Communication Engineering. The conference is designed to showcase the technical expertise of emerging technologists, research scholars, and distinguished representatives from academia and industry. The primary goal of the conference is to gather leading research professionals from around the world to foster collaboration and exchange cutting-edge knowledge.

Scope of the Conference

The first international conference on 2025 Implication of AI for Sustainable Innovations in Global Transition (IASIGT-2025) aims to provide cutting-edge knowledge in multidisciplinary aspects for the sustainable development of the nation. Artificial intelligence (AI) is a powerful, cutting-edge, and booming domain that, when infused into various domains, has the potential to make significant advancements and foster innovations in sustainable development. This international conference aims to bridge the knowledge of future engineers, researchers, academicians, and industry experts, providing insights into upcoming ways, means, and methods using AI technology, which can benefit global development by considering sustainable environments for the future.

Objectives

  • Foster collaboration among artificial intelligence experts and sustainability experts to formulate groundbreaking solutions for global challenges.
  • To find the various innovative methods of applications of artificial intelligence in energy conversion, electrification, integration, design optimization, and energy storage for the acceleration of global transitions towards sustainable economies and green energy sources.
  • To find the various opportunities for Developing Sustainable Smart Cities with the aid of Artificial Intelligence.
  • Identify opportunities for AI to enhance resource efficiency and reduce environmental impact.
  • An Artificial Intelligence Approach to Enhancing Safety and Empowerment.
  • Analyze case studies of successful AI-driven sustainable innovations in various sectors.

About Sri Eshwar College of Engineering

Sri Eshwar College of Engineering is an Autonomous Institution, approved by AICTE, New Delhi, and affiliated with Anna University, Chennai. Established in 2008, the institution is dedicated to providing a holistic approach to education, focusing on delivering quality education. Sri Eshwar College of Engineering offers ten undergraduate programs and three postgraduate programs. The campus is located near Kinathukadavu, approximately 25 km from Coimbatore Railway Station.
