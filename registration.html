Registration Policy

  • Registration fee does not include accommodation.
  • Accommodation is available on a separate chargeable basis.
  • Registration fees cover the Conference kit, Proceedings (softcopy), E-Certificate, lunch, and refreshment for both days.
  • Authors can present their papers through Virtual / Video conferencing.
  • For cancellations received before or on August 10, 2025, a 50% refund of the registration fee will be granted. After August 10, 2025, no refund can be given.
